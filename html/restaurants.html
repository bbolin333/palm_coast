Mamaling Asian Street Food

Asian street food recipes bring the lively streets of South East Asia to your table, with Vietnamese pho, Japanese ramen, Thai lemongrass curry, Szechuan mini taco and much more.

La Piazza Cafe

La Piazza Café in Palm Coast’s European Village blends old-world Italian tradition with modern culinary flair. Owners Diane and Larry Lass, originally from New Jersey, opened the restaurant after moving to Flagler County, even bringing family from Rome to help launch it. Chef Josh, a Culinary Institute of America graduate, adds a contemporary touch to their menu of high-quality, in-house prepared steaks and seafood. With a loyal staff and a family-style atmosphere, La Piazza offers guests both authentic flavors and warm hospitality.
